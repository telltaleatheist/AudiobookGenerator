In fact, he was so interested that he learned the Sanskrit language so he could read the epic stories in their original language. The epic stories commonly centered around a group of heroic figures they called Aryans. Germans called them der ubermensch or the Superman. The word Aryan is of Indo-European origin but was exclusively used in the Indo-Iranian region. The meaning changed over time. But by the fourth century, it was commonly used to reference a high social caste in the area. It's often rendered as noble in modern scholarly analysis.
